Meet Pastor Carlyle & Family

A Ministry Rooted in Family

Pastor Carlyle's ministry is a shared, family endeavor. He is dedicated to serving the communities of Adamsville and Fairfield, believing strongly in building faith-based homes and vibrant church families.

He is supported by his wife, a licensed **Real Estate Agent** and experienced **Teacher**, who brings her professional and educational skills to the church. She also serves in the crucial role of the **Adventurer Leader**, guiding the spiritual and social development of our young people.

Together, they are raising **three children** who are actively involved in the life and outreach of the church, exemplifying the value of serving God together.

Children Focused Christian Club

A dedicated ministry designed to nurture faith, character, and service in our youngest members.

Our Mission & Focus

  • **Fun with Purpose:** Interactive Bible stories, crafts, and games centered around Christ's life.
  • **Character Building:** Focused lessons on values like kindness, honesty, and forgiveness.
  • **Safety First:** All club activities are run by background-checked volunteers in a secure environment.
  • **Service Projects:** Age-appropriate activities to teach community outreach and service.

Ages & Schedule

Age Groups:

Explorers (Ages 4-7) & Adventurers (Ages 8-12)

Meeting Time:

Every 1st and 3rd Sunday of the month, 3:00 PM - 5:00 PM (Rotating between Adamsville and Fairfield)

Registration:

Spaces are limited. Please contact the main office to register your child.
